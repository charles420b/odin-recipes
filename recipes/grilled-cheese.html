Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of slice bread.
    Place bread butter-side down in hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
    Butter a second slice of bread on one side ad place butter-side up on top of cheese.

  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking
    until cheese is melted. Reapeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
