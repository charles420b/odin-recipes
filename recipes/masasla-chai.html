Masala Chai

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoon loose leaf black tea
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 clove
  • 1 inch knob of ginger, chopped into slices
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black peppercorn, freshly cracked
  • 1/2 cinnamon stick
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoon sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Add the whole spices to a mortar and crush them lightly into smaller pieces.
  2. In a small saucepan on high heat, pour in the water and add the spices.
    Bring the water to a boil and add the tea bags or leaves. Lower the heat to a
    medium to low setting and simmer for two minutes, or until the tea leaves
    and spices have released flavor and color. Then, pour in the milk.
